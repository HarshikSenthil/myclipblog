Google Image Search Is Broken for GetYarn.io Clips

(Proof, Timeline & Call for Google’s Attention)

Last Updated: June 2025

🚨 Summary

Since early 2024, Google Image Search results for GetYarn.io — a widely-used video clip platform — have progressively degraded. Search queries that once returned relevant, previewable video thumbnails now show broken, irrelevant, or missing results.

This post outlines:

  • A detailed timeline of bugs
  • Visual evidence
  • Steps to reproduce
  • A final request for Google's Search and Engineering teams

🕐 Timeline of Bugs (April 2024 → Today)

Date Issue Description
April 2024 Out-of-Order / Jumbled Results Yarn clips began appearing in unrelated or random queries, breaking visual navigation.
Nov 2024 Incorrect or Replaced Thumbnails Thumbnails no longer match clips — visual mismatches are widespread.
Jan 2025 Massive Image Loss + Filter Failure Key filters like “Clip” and “Quotes” stopped working. Most image previews vanished.
May 2025 Fully Broken Rendering Image search returns almost no valid thumbnails. Relevance is gone.
